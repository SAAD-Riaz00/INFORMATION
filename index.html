BASIC INFORMATION ABOUT TOYOTA SUPRA

The Toyota Supra is a sports car and grand tourer manufactured by the Toyota Motor Corporation1. The name "supra" is derived from the Latin prefix, meaning "above", "to surpass" or "go beyond"1. The initial four generations of the Supra were produced from 1978 to 20021. The latest model of the Supra has a 3.0-litre engine and twin-scroll turbocharger producing 285kW and 500Nm2. It has won multiple awards, including Drive Sports Car of the Year 2020 and West Wheels Sports Car of the Year 20192.
